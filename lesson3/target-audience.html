Target Audience

Description

The target audience of this site will consist of the community members from the Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven areas. These community members are mostly senior couples, and young families.

The senior couples are set in their ways and most likely grew up farming in the area. They don't have a lot of experience with technology and will need all the help they can get.

The younger families are new to the area. They don't know a lot about the weather in Idaho, but are interested to learn about their new town and what they can expect when it comes to weather.

Personas

Persona 1: Frank and Evelyn Barnes

Age: 74-76
Hometown: Soda Springs, ID
Current Town: Soda Springs, ID
Occupation: Retired
Goals: This senior couple knows what they like and won't stray from it. They visit Proper Doppler to figure out if they should brave the snow or not, whether they can wear their favorite jean shorts and sandals, or if they need to beat the heat inside while they watch Jeopardy.
Tech Savvy Rating: 2/10

Persona 2: Chandler and Remingtn Baumann

Age: 21-24
Hometown: Winter Springs, FL
Current Town: Preston, ID
Occupation: Business Owner
Goals: This young couple is new to the area. They visit Proper Doppler often so they can get familiar with the past and present weather patterns of Preston, Idaho. They are used to shorts and a t-shirt everyday, but need Proper Doppler information to decide what they should wear, and if they need to bring a jacket or a coat.
Tech Savvy Rating: 8/10

Scenarios

  1. What should I wear today?
  2. Is the weather good enough to go garage saling, or should I shop online?
  3. What was the weather like around this time last year?
  4. Are hail storms a frequent occurance?
  5. Should I close my store for the safety of my employees?
